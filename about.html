About

Bio

Hi, I’m Annemarie Schaepman, a UI-Designer in France with a passion for nature and an affinity for visual design. Nature is where I feel most at home, drop me on a mountain or in some woods, and I am a happy camper. It’s the same with visual design. I could easily tell you all about me through images and illustrations, but to put it in words well … results in this. However, I am never scared to take on a challenge, be it monkey wrangling, opinionated clients, Monday mornings, or writing a bio. I was born in the Netherlands, grew up in Germany, went to an American highschool and am currently living in France. For ten years, I have been a photographer helping businesses create a visual presence. In 2019, I became a mother and had the opportunity to take a year off. It became a time of self-reflection in which I realized that I would like to continue my passion but in a different direction. This realization led me to become a certified UI-Designer with CareerFoundry in 2021. For me waking up in the morning and working with beautiful images, fonts, illustrations to create functional and attractive interfaces is ever so satisfying. If you’d like to collaborate with me or have a chat, then send me a message at annemarie.schaepman (at) gmail.com
